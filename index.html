What A Mess Archives is an ongoing collaborative archive, developed by Pedro Gil Farias and Hugo Pilate, exploring the untapped narrative potential of the urban fabric through a collection of 3D samples of the urban environment.

About

This archive documents artistic 3D scans of public spaces across different cities, preserving moments in digital form.

Our collection includes individual specimens, curated aggregates, and various manifestations where these scans find new life in exhibitions and projects.
